Giving and receiving feedback is hard. People can feel pressured to give you empty positive comments or give in to biases that aren't directly related to the work you do.

Work With Me Again is a simple tool that gives people you've worked with before, the opportunity to anonymously say whether they'd work with you again, as well as optionally leave you comments on why they would make that decision.

They are also asked questions about in what capacity you worked together, gender identity, ethnic identity, and age when you worked together. This data is then analyzed to help you have the opportunity to change how you interact at work in the future.
